## Overview

MYCOBEANS addresses the emerging risk of mycotoxins in beans and legumes used as alternative plant protein sources, combining advanced analytical diagnostics, toxicological assessment and biotechnological mitigation across the supply chain. 
- **WP1 — Advanced diagnostics for mycotoxin detection** (Lead: **TU**, Month **1–42**)  
- **WP3 — Biotechnological mitigation** (Lead: **NSTDA**, Month **6–48**)
- **WP4 — Knowledge transfer and public engagement** (Lead: **QUB**, Month **1–48**)  
- **WP5 — Project management, dissemination and exploitation** (Lead: **UNIPR**, Month **1–48**)  

➡️ Full details are available on the dedicated **Work Packages** page.
