## Aim

MYCOBEANS aims to strengthen collaboration and excellence across academic and non-academic organisations to address the **emerging risk of mycotoxins in beans and legumes** used as alternative plant protein sources, supporting **food safety** and **supply-chain resilience** under climate change.
