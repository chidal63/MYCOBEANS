**Consortium kick-off meeting to launch MYCOBEANS activities**

The MYCOBEANS project officially launched with its kick-off meeting, bringing together partners from academia, research institutes, and industry across Europe and beyond.
The meeting set the scientific and strategic foundations for addressing mycotoxin risks in pulses under a changing climate.
Key objectives, work packages, and collaborative pathways were aligned from the outset.
The focus: translating advanced analytical science into practical tools for food safety.
A strong start for a project built on rigor, integration, and impact.
